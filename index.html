Chinese comfort meals

MALATANG

麻辣烫 (málàtàng/maa4 laat6 tong3)

Sep 10,2025

Malatang is a Sichuan-style Chinese street food and a form of "personal" hot pot where you choose your ingredients, which are then cooked to order in a flavorful, spicy, and numbing broth known as "mala". The name combines the Chinese words "ma" (numbing), "la" (spicy), "tang" (soup/broth) referring to the Sichuan peppercorns and chili peppers that give the broth its signature tingle and heat.

EGG DROP SOUP

蛋花湯 (dànhuātāng/daan2 faa1 tong1)

Sep 11, 2025

Often made when sick or in colder weather, egg drop soup (also known as egg flower soup), is a comforting Chinese soup of wispy beaten eggs in chicken broth. Condiments such as black or white pepper, and finely chopped scallions and tofu, are commonly added to the soup.

WONTON NOODLE SOUP

云吞面 (yúntūnmiàn/wan4 tan1 min6)

Sep 12,2025

Wonton noodle soup is a classic, comforting Cantonese dish featuring a savory broth, plump meat-and-shrimp wontons, chewy egg noodles, and leafy greens like bok choy. This meal is a staple in Chinese cuisine, particularly in Hong Kong, and offers a perfect balance of textures, flavors, and comfort, making it a popular choice for a complete, warming meal.
